How it works

Three steps. Zero real money. Not gambling.

STEP 01

🌍

Browse or create

Scroll through live predictions or drop your own in a friend group. Six prediction types: Yes/No, Who, Over/Under, When, Most Likely To, and Rate.

STEP 02

🗳️

Vote with Cred

Spend virtual Cred on your prediction. Watch the percentages shift in real-time. See how your group compares to the market. Cred has no real-world value — it's points, like XP.

STEP 03

🔥

Called it — or not

When the truth comes out, correct predictions earn Cred. Wrong ones lose it. Your scores update. The leaderboard shifts. No cash changes hands — ever.
